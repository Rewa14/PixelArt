Find a reliable Site or Program

  • To make Pixel Art you first need to find a site or program to make art on it. Below are some sites that I recomend:

    • Piskel
    • Pixie
    • Pixel Artor
    • PIXEL'D app

    Also you will need to have a color wheel type site open such as,

    • Computer Hope
    • Color Blender
    • Learn the mechanics of the program

  • This step is not necessary but is highly reccomended since really knowing and being comfortable with the program will give you an extra edge when "drawing."

    • Decide on dimensons of your art

  • This can always be changed over time but knowing an idea of how detailed you want your drawing is helpful.

    • Find an Image to base it off of

  • If you are making a whole new person or a character that already exists, having an image to go off of is helpful. Especially when you need the to know the clothes, objects, and colors you are going to need.

    • Start to Art
